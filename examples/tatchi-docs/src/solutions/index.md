# Solutions

Use Tatchi to ship embedded wallet and signing experiences for security-sensitive applications.

## By Use Case

### Consumer Apps

Passkey-native wallet flows without extension installs or popup detours.

### Stablecoin Payments

Embedded transaction confirmation and signing in checkout and payment journeys.

### Treasury and Payouts

Policy-based transaction approvals for internal transfers, vendor payouts, and operations.

### Recovery and Device Linking

Cross-device account continuity through secure linking and recovery primitives.

## By Team

### Product Teams

Composable wallet UX patterns that help reduce onboarding and signing friction.

### Security Teams

SecureConfirm and threshold signing controls for high-integrity transaction boundaries.

### Platform Teams

Reusable signing and wallet infrastructure for multiple products and environments.
